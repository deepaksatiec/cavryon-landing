Frequently Asked Questions

Does Cavryon replace my ERP or accounting software?

No. Cavryon works alongside your existing system. Just export your GL to Excel from QuickBooks, NetSuite, Xero, or Sage — Cavryon analyzes it and returns a flagged workbook. Nothing changes in your ERP.

Is my GL data secure?

Yes. All uploads are encrypted end-to-end. Your data is never stored beyond your session, never shared with third parties, and never used to train AI models.

How long does it take to get results?

Upload your GL export and get back a flagged Excel workbook in under 2 minutes. No setup, no onboarding, no IT involvement required.

What accounting systems does it work with?

Cavryon works with any system that can export to Excel — QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, NetSuite, Xero, and Sage Intacct. If you can export a GL detail report, Cavryon can analyze it.

Do I need IT or any integration setup?

No. There is no integration, no API connection, and no IT involvement needed. Export your GL to Excel, upload the file, and get results. That's it.

What exactly does Cavryon flag?

Cavryon detects duplicate transactions, vendor-to-account mismatches, missing department or class coding, potential prepaid candidates, unusual month-over-month account activity, and variance drivers. Each flag is color-coded by severity in the returned workbook.
